Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really built by AI?

AI builds the foundation — layout, initial copy, and images. Then, our human design team reviews and refines every detail to ensure it meets our quality standards and your brand vision.

Can I make changes after launch?

Yes! Depending on your plan, you get a certain number of monthly update hours included in your retainer. For major changes, we're always available for custom project work.

How does the 48-hour delivery work?

Once you submit your brief, our AI gets to work immediately. We usually have a first draft ready for you within 24 hours, leaving another 24 hours for your review and our final refinements.

Do I own the website?

Absolutely. While we host and maintain it for you to keep things simple, the website and its content are 100% your property.
